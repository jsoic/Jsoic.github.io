3D Bar Charts

To be clear, all representations of 3D bar charts are not innately bad, I simply hold the opinion that they can sometimes be misleading. I believe the design is intended for aesthetic purposes as much of the data typically displayed in 3D bar charts can usually be done in a 2D format.

3D bar charts can usually be unhelpful to the observer and introduce several issues:

  • Visually overwhelming with too much information
  • Difficult to differntiate comparisons
  • Inaccurate data interpretations
  • Inefficient with space usage

Ideally, data visualization concepts should be presented in an efficient and appealing way wihthout loosing its accurate integrity. In conclusion, visualizations such as 3D bar charts fail to maintain accuracy, ease of interpretation, and emit uneccassary clutter which can be confusing for the audience.
